# PDF Craft Cloud

Turn your PDF documents into formats that are easier to read, edit, and share. PDF Craft Cloud automatically handles the conversion, so you can focus on using your content the way you want.

## What Can You Do?

### Turn PDFs into E-Books
Convert any PDF document into an EPUB file that you can read on your Kindle, iPad, or any e-reader device. Perfect for reading research papers, reports, or books on the go with better text reflowing and font size control.

### Convert PDFs to Editable Text
Transform PDF documents into Markdown format, making it easy to edit content, extract information, or repurpose the text for your own projects. Great for taking notes from articles, adapting content for presentations, or creating documentation.

## How It Works

PDF Craft Cloud provides one simple tool called **PDF Converter** that does all the heavy lifting:

1. **Point to Your PDF**: Tell it where your PDF is - either by providing a web link or uploading a file from your computer
2. **Choose Your Format**: Pick whether you want an EPUB e-book or Markdown text file
3. **Let It Run**: The converter automatically handles everything - it processes your PDF and gives you back the converted file

That's it! No complicated steps or settings to worry about.

## Smart Features

The converter includes intelligent features that work automatically in the background:

- **Progress Monitoring**: Watch your conversion progress in real-time
- **Error Handling**: If parts of your PDF are difficult to read (like scanned pages or unusual formatting), the converter can skip those parts and continue with the rest
- **Automatic Processing**: Just provide your PDF and desired format - everything else is handled for you

## Getting Started

Simply drag the "PDF Converter" block into your workspace and connect it with your PDF source. The tool handles everything from there, delivering your converted file right where you need it.

No technical knowledge required - if you can provide a PDF location and choose a format, you're ready to go.
